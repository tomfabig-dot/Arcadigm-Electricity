## Design Context

### Users
Australian C&I (commercial & industrial) operators with multi-site electricity spend — manufacturers, retail chains, cold-store/logistics — typically an operations manager, facilities lead, or CFO at an SME with $200k–$1m+ annual electricity bills. They evaluate Arcadigm during work hours on desktop. They are not energy experts; they've been burned by one-off audits that produced dusty PDFs. They want continuous visibility and a partner who executes, not another dashboard.

### Brand Personality
Grounded, rigorous, Australian-plainspoken. Sounds like a trusted engineer-operator, not a SaaS startup. Evidence-led, zero hype, no exclamation marks, no "Elevate/Seamless/Unleash". Confident restraint. Treats the reader as an adult.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial-industrial, warm paper + deep green.** Cream-paper background tinted toward the brand green; deep-green (#164d35) as the primary brand hue; typography does the heavy lifting with a distinctive display face paired with a refined reading face. Asymmetric, left-aligned compositions. Data presented as editorial artefact — numbers treated like a measurement report, not dashboard chrome. Subtle paper grain. Light mode.

**Anti-references:** purple gradients, glowy neon accents, centred hero + "Start for free" template, 3-equal-card feature rows, Inter/Space Grotesk/DM Sans, generic drop-shadow cards, emoji icons, AI slop language.

### Design Principles
1. **Editorial, not app-chrome.** Lay the page like a considered publication — whitespace, typographic contrast, asymmetric grids. No card-on-card nesting.
2. **Data as artefact.** Real numbers, real NMI fragments, real site names presented with the gravity of a measurement — mono for numerals, paper-like chart treatment, no sparkline decoration.
3. **Plainspoken Australian.** Short declarative sentences, concrete verbs, no startup puffery. If it sounds like a pitch deck, rewrite it.
4. **Motion is evidence of life, not spectacle.** Perpetual micro-motion on live-data elements (breathing status, metric carousel) is welcome; scroll-hijack, parallax, and bounce easing are banned.
5. **Deep green is rare and earns its place.** Cream + ink dominate; the brand green appears on CTAs, key numerals, and one editorial accent per section — never as a gradient, never as a glow.

### Technical Constraints
- React 18 + Vite + Tailwind v3 (keep the stack).
- Existing routes stay: `/`, `/about`, `/why-us`, `/track-record`, `/process`, `/sign-in`, `/sign-up`, `/portal/*`.
- Light mode only for marketing.
- Deep green `#164d35` locked as primary brand hue.
- WCAG AA contrast minimums; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
